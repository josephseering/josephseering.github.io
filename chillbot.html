Chillbot moderation study

This study, which focuses on understanding the potential utility of private, non-punitive nudges in correcting user behavior, is being run in the HCI group in the Computer Science Department at Stanford University under the supervision of Michael Bernstein and Joseph Seering. We are recruiting moderators who are interested in adding a bot to their Discord servers for a minimum of two weeks, after which they will participate in brief interviews about how the bot was used. Moderators will be compensated with an Amazon gift code for $50 (or local currency equivalent) upon completion of the study.
